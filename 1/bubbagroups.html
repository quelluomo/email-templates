Bubba Gump Shrimp Co.

Bubba Gump Shrimp Co., a tribute to the much-loved 1994 smash hit movie Forrest Gump and the USA's favourite seafood restaurant can be found in London's iconic Trocadero building in Piccadilly Circus with the capacity of 400 seated and 650 standing.


As Europe's first and only flagship store, Bubba Gump Shrimp Co. gloriously captures the vivacious spirit and charm which made Forrest Gump a smash hit in the first place, featuring southern down-home decor, reminiscent of the film's setting in Alabama.

Don't forget to have a seat on the bus stop bench by the entrance, featuring that famous box of chocolates and pose for an Instagram worthy picture!

The popular Run Forrest Run and Stop Forrest Stop licence plates are on every table, letting servers know when they are required, guaranteeing you a top notch service every time.

Food is our hero!

Food is the hero at Bubba Gump Shrimp Company Co. We serve food that makes our employees proud and keeps our guests coming back.
Bubba says, ‘SHRIMP IS THE FRUIT OF THE SEA’! Shrimp, ‘the fruit of the sea’, is of course our specialty and we serve it up in over 20 different ways!
We’re also known for our top quality seafood dishes including our traditional English style ‘Captain’s Fish & Chips’.
After something meatier? Our home-style cookin’ includes succulent steaks and grills as well as our award winning baby back ribs.
Children of all ages love our fun kid’s meals and everyone will want to save room for dessert.
